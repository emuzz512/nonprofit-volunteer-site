Why I'm Here

Over the past years, my career has focused on process improvement and design, with a healthy dose of untangling complexity, aligning cross-functional teams, and designing better systems for long-term sustainability.

Most of that work has been inside high-growth tech, but I started in nonprofits—and regardless of the field, I've always carried a deep interest in how systems serve (or fail) our community members.

With the potential lapse in SNAP benefits and the rising strain on service organizations, I've felt compelled to engage and serve. I'm not seeking a job, consulting role, or side hustle. I'm offering to volunteer my professional skillset to organizations working to meet rising needs with limited resources.
